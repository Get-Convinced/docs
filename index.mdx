Convinced is a personalized B2B sales widget. Partners push target-account intel through the campaign API, and the widget loads with `?c=<token>` to ground the conversation in that intel — opener, discovery questions, case studies, and competitive positioning all keyed off the account in front of it.

This site covers how to integrate the campaign API, what intel the agent uses, and how to embed the widget in your outbound flow.

## What is campaign mode?

A **campaign** groups target companies under a stable slug. For each target you upsert a row carrying either a fully-formed `intel` object (partner-intel mode) or rep notes for Convinced to synthesize (auto-enrich mode). Each target gets a `widgetToken` that you embed in your outbound link as `?c=<token>`. When the visitor lands, the widget resolves the token, loads the intel, and the agent runs the conversation grounded in that account's context — not a generic pitch.
